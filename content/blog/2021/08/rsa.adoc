== RSA

Euler's theorem:
[stem]
++++
a^{\phi(n)}\equiv 1(\mod n) \ where \gcd(a,n) = 1
++++
Euler's totient function:
[stem]
++++
let\ p\ is\ a\ prime\ number, \\
q\ is\ another\ prime\ number. \\
let\ n = p*q \\
\phi(p)=p-1 \\
\phi(q)=q-1 \\
\phi(n)=(p-1)*(q-1) \\
\phi(a*b)=\phi(a)*\phi(b) \ where \ gcd(a,b)=1 \\
a^{p-1} \equiv 1 (\mod p) \\
a^{q-1} \equiv 1 (\mod q) \\
a^{\phi(n)} \equiv 1 (\mod n) \\
a^{(p-1)*(q-1)} \equiv 1(\mod p*q)
++++
